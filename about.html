Our Story

Founded on September 17, 2025, Quantam Core Studio began with a shared passion for interactive media and robust software solutions. Based in Bongaon, West Bengal, India, we have grown from an ambitious startup into a dedicated hub for high-quality game development and mobile application engineering. Our vision is to build a strong global presence while delivering exceptional digital experiences.
